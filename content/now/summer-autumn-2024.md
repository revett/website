💦🔥 After two fulfilling years at [incident.io](http://incident.io/), I've shifted my focus to freelance while building my own digital products. Check out [@8thpark](https://8thpark.com) for product releases and [@cdrev](https://www.instagram.com/cdrev/) for travel updates.

I'm loving my new (but really very old) Kindle 3, thanks to [@andy_provan](https://x.com/andy_provan)'s persistent recommendations to move away from paper. It has dramatically improved my reading habits and reduced time spent on social media. Follow my reading journey on [Goodreads](https://www.goodreads.com/user/show/164650365-charlie-revett).

My travel plans include Spain 🇪🇸, Austria 🇦🇹, Croatia 🇭🇷, and Montreal 🇨🇦. I'm also looking forward to Glastonbury and Come Bye festivals this summer.

I'm becoming more involved in the indie dev/maker community through [WIP.co](https://wip.co/). You'll also find me maintaining a more consistent presence on [X](https://twitter.com/revcd), sharing insights and connecting with fellow creators.

For the coming months, my goals are to explore diverse working styles, embrace slow travel, nurture my involvement in the maker community, and maintain (or even boost) my current reading momentum.
