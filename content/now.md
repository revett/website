## Winter / Spring 2025

Covering December 1, 2024 to May 31, 2025.

![](/images/now-hero.jpg)

After six months of freelancing following my time at [incident.io](http://incident.io/), I’m off on a six month sabbatical. Freelancing brought a lot of freedom — the autonomy to drive pace within a small team, especially in the fast-paced world of crypto. It was a refreshing change, but it’s clear that it’s not my long-term path.

To kick things off, I’m spending ten weeks travelling solo across Japan. My focus is on slow travel, intentionally veering off the well-worn "Golden Route", and immersing myself in Japan’s hiking trails. It's a purposeful slowdown, part of a broader effort to step back from the everyday.

I’m also addressing a long-standing ITB knee injury, by reintroducing running into my routine to explore whilst travelling. Surprisingly, photography has become a joy too. Having felt uninspired by the familiar streets of London, I’m now capturing daily.

Next is a road trip through Kenya, Tanzania, Zambia, Namibia, to South Africa. From there, I’ll cross the Atlantic to drive coast-to-coast from San Francisco to New York in a van, taking the scenic route.

After spending a decade in London’s startup ecosystem, I’m grateful for the ability to pause. Space to reflect on where I want to put down roots next, what I want to focus on in my career, and how to approach the ever-changing landscape of AI and its effects. This is a moment to recharge and recalibrate.
